A car is a road vehicle used to carry passengers (people). Cars usually have four wheels(round things which turn in order to lead to movement), and an engine or motor to make them move. A large amount of cars were made during the 20th century.A car is a vehicle that has wheels, carries a small number of passengers, and is moved by an engine or a motor. Cars are also called automobiles or motor vehicles. Trucks and buses are motor vehicles as well. However, trucks and buses are larger than cars, and they carry heavier loads.
